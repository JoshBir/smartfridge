About SmartFridge

SmartFridge is a kitchen management application designed to help you reduce food waste and make cooking easier.

Our Mission

We believe that with better organisation and smart suggestions, everyone can reduce food waste and enjoy cooking more. SmartFridge helps you keep track of what's in your fridge, alerts you before items expire, and suggests recipes based on what you have available.

Key Features
  • Ingredient Tracking: Keep a digital inventory of your fridge with expiry date tracking
  • Visual Alerts: Green, amber, and red indicators show item freshness at a glance
  • Recipe Suggestions: Get personalised recipe ideas based on your available ingredients
  • AI-Powered: Optional AI integration for creative recipe suggestions
  • Favourite Sites: Organise and tag your favourite cooking websites
  • Multi-User Support: Each user has their own private inventory
Security

Your data security is our priority. SmartFridge implements industry-standard security practices including:

  • Secure password hashing with bcrypt
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Secure session management
  • Content Security Policy headers
  • HTTPS enforcement in production
Open Source

SmartFridge is open source software released under the MIT licence. Contributions are welcome!
